Hesburgh Sidewalk Closure

The sidewalks on the northeast side of the library will be closed April 11-13th. Please avoid this area by walking around the south side of the library.

Earth Hour

The university will join others across the globe by turning off the lights shining on the dome and the library for one hour at 8:30 pm on April 11, 2019. Please join us in being environmentally friendly. Reduce. Reuse. Recycle.

The Bald and The Beautiful

The annual event will take place April 3-5th on the Hagerty stage in the Duncan Student Center. The event benefits St. Baldrick's, Beacon Memorial Children's Hospital, and Children with Hair Loss. You can shave your head, donate 8" of hair, or purchase colorful extensions.

Giving Day

Notre Dame's annual giving day will be on April 25, 2019. Please consider donating to organizations who have positively contributed to your experience at Notre Dame.
